# Restaurant Sales Dashboard – Power BI Project

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of restaurant sales performance and customer behavior. The project focuses on transforming raw transactional data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations and key performance indicators.

The dashboard highlights important metrics such as Total Revenue, Total Number of Customers, and Average Transaction Value, enabling stakeholders to monitor overall business performance at a glance.

Users can analyze revenue trends across different days of the week and identify peak business periods. The dashboard includes a time-based analysis that compares sales performance during Lunch and Dinner hours, helping management understand customer dining preferences.

A detailed revenue breakdown by gender provides insights into customer demographics and purchasing patterns. Interactive filters allow users to explore the data dynamically and focus on specific periods for deeper analysis.

Key Features:

* Total Revenue KPI tracking
* Customer count analysis
* Average Transaction Value monitoring
* Revenue analysis by day of the week
* Revenue contribution by dining time
* Customer segmentation by gender
* Interactive filtering and drill-down capabilities
* Business performance trend visualization
* Clean and user-friendly dashboard design
* Data-driven decision support

Business Insights Generated:

* Identification of highest revenue-generating days
* Understanding customer traffic patterns
* Analysis of dining preferences across time periods
* Evaluation of customer demographic contributions
* Monitoring of sales performance trends
* Recognition of opportunities for revenue growth

Tools & Technologies Used:

* Power BI
* Data Modeling
* DAX Measures
* Power Query
* Data Visualization
* Business Intelligence Reporting

The dashboard is designed to help restaurant managers optimize operations, improve customer experience, and maximize revenue through data-driven insights.
